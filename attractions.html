Top Attractions

Four must-visit places for first-time travelers and panda lovers in Chengdu.

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

Chengdu's most iconic attraction where visitors can observe giant pandas and learn about conservation efforts.

Jinli Ancient Street

A historic street lined with local snacks, crafts, and lantern-lit architecture that reflects Chengdu's cultural heritage.

Wuhou Shrine

A major historical landmark dedicated to the Three Kingdoms era, known for peaceful courtyards and traditional temple design.

People's Park

A popular local park with tea houses, gardens, and walking paths where visitors can experience everyday Chengdu city life.
